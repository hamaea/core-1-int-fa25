i really want a cat

i've always wanted a cat. i've seen studies that said that having a cat reduces rates of anxiety and depression! a fix all built into a little cat. when i was 5, i visited my family's home in japan, which had a little black cat. the cat chased me everywhere and tried to scratch me even though i wanted to play with it. i remember my dad scolding me for provoking it. despite this, i always wanted and still want a cat.

paper: 1 square sheet. all we need for our cat!

step 1

start with the white side of the paper facing up. fold it diagonally.

step 2

fold the left and right corners towards the bottom corner along the dotted lines.

step 3

valley fold the top flaps along the dotted lines to create the cat’s ears.

step 4

fold the top corner down along the dotted line.

step 5

turn the model over, then fold the bottom layer up along the dotted line.

step 6

fold the tip of the top layer down along the dotted line to create the cat’s nose.

step 7

lift the bottom corner and tuck it inside the model. fold at the same level as the crease created in step 4.

fin.

our cat now!
