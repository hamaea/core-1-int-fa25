let's send a letter!

origami envelope

unsent letters versus unsent texts

"the post office" by jason polan is one of my favorite books that i found recently. gathering imagery of art that they were sent, they compiled into their book and created this "post-office" style book. i've grown up surrounded by digital email and messaging, it's so interesting to try and send real letters now.

paper: 1 square sheet. (kraft / kami works. just make clean creases.)

step 1

start with the white side up. fold in half diagonally, then unfold.

step 2

fold in half diagonally the other way, then unfold.

step 3

fold two opposite corners inward so their tips meet at the center point.

step 4

fold the top corner down to the center point, but only pinch lightly where it hits the diagonal (tiny crease). then unfold.

step 5

rotate the paper 45° counterclockwise.

step 6

fold the bottom corner up to meet the pinch mark from step 4, then unfold.

step 7

fold the bottom corner up to meet the crease made in step 6.

step 8

fold the bottom left section upward, lining its left edge up with the crease made in step 6.

step 9

repeat on the right side.

step 10

unfold the folds made in steps 7 to 9.

step 11

fold along the dotted lines, using the existing creases as guides (this is the “most annoying” fold—go slow).

step 12

fold the bottom corner up, using the creases made in step 11 as guides.

step 13

collapse the paper along the existing creases (use the diagram logic: valley vs mountain folds).

step 14

open the top left flap while releasing the one trapped in the bottom left corner (keep the top flap raised).

step 15

close the top left flap over the flap beneath it. the mountain fold follows an existing crease; the valley fold makes a new crease.

step 16

repeat steps 14 to 15 on the top right flap.

step 17

make a mountain fold at the midpoint of the square (dotted line). swing the square backward without creasing through it.

step 18

fold the entire bottom flap upwards along the existing crease.

step 19

gently open the left flap trapped under the bottom left flap. don’t flatten it yet.

step 20

tuck the corner of the flap underneath the square.

step 21

repeat steps 19 to 20 on the right side.

step 22

fold the top corner down to meet the bottom edge, then unfold.

step 23

fold the top corner down to meet the crease made in step 22.

fin.

time to write your letter :)
