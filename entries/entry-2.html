origami crane

my sister and i grew up with the story of sadako sasaki and the thousand paper cranes. the story was that sadako sasaki had been diagnosed with leukemia, and folded a thousand paper cranes for a wish in accordance with japanese folklore. the story instills hope in millions of japanese, and it's something i think of all the time when i'm feeling down. hope never hurts.
