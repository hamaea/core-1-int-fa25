let's make a turtle

step one

start with the green side of the paper facing up. fold it in half horizontally, crease it well then unfold it.

step 2

fold it in half vertically, crease it well then unfold it.

step 3

flip it over.

step 4

fold it in half diagonally, getting a crease, and unfold it.

step 5

fold it in half diagonally from the other side, crease it well then unfold.

step 6

now, use both hands to press the two middle points on the right and left edges. then push it down to make the two middle points meet the center point of the bottom edge. flatten the fold to get a triangle.

step 7

fold the right edge to the left along the dotted line, ensure that the right edge meets the middle point of the bottom edge, and the right corner goes over the bottom edge. crease it well then unfold it. repeat this for the left edge.

step 8

fold the left and right corners of the top layer up to the top point diagonally, crease them well, and unfold them.

step 9

bring up the right edge and the right part of the bottom edge along the line connecting the right corner and the intersection point formed by the two creases you just made.

step 10

press down the middle part of the right edge, shape the right corner, and then fold it up to the top point, ensuring the bottom right edge aligns with the vertical crease.

step 11

repeat steps 9 to 10 for the left side.

step 12

flip the paper over.

step 13

repeat steps 7 to 10 for this side.

step 14

take the top right corner, and fold it down along the dotted line.

step 15

flip the model over.

step 16

similarly, fold the top left corner down along the dotted line.

step 17

now take the bottom left edge, and fold it up to align with the horizontal line connecting the left and right points.

step 18

take the corner you made in the previous step, and fold it to the left along the dotted line.

step 19

still the same corner, fold it down, then lift it to make it stand.

step 20

view it from the bottom left to see this flap.

step 21

press the flap from its top to open it, then flatten it.

step 22

flip the model over.

step 23

in the same manner as you did in step 17, fold the bottom right edge up to align with the horizontal line connecting the left and right points.

step 24

then fold the corner you made in the previous step to the right along the dotted line.

step 25

still this same corner, fold it down along the dotted line, then lift it to make it stand.

step 26

view it from the bottom right.

step 27

press the top point of this flap to open it, then flatten it, as you did in step 21.

step 28

now take the left flap, and fold it down to the left along the dotted line.

step 29

flip the paper over.

step 30

in the same manner, fold the right flap down to the right along the dotted line, ensuring it overlaps with the flap you made in step 28.

step 31

view it from the top, and make the top two flaps together at the top, and the bottom two flaps together at the bottom. this will change the model from opening on the left and right to opening on the top and bottom. then lay it flat.

step 32

fold the bottom left and right corners of the turtle‚Äôs body a little backward.

step 33

the corresponding two points on the bottom layer should be folded in the same way.

step 34

fold the right flipper down along the dotted line, then lift it 90 degrees.

step 35

view it from the right opening.

step 36

press the flap you made in step 34, that is the turtle‚Äôs right flipper, then open and flatten it.

step 37

repeat steps 34 to 36 for its left flipper.

step 38

turn over the model.

step 39

fold the top point down along the dotted line.

step 40

then fold it up along the dotted line.

step 41

open the bottom right corner of the top part, then push this corner, and flatten it.

step 42

repeat this for the bottom left corner.

step 43

fold the top point down a little to form the turtle‚Äôs head.

step 44

flip the model over. this is the final shape of the origami turtle.
