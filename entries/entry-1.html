let's make a turtle

the first time i went back to japan in years was in fourth grade. we visited my aunt's house, where my sister's pet turtle from YEARS ago was still there. won at a carnival, the turtle grew to be as big as me.

step 1

white side up: fold in half vertically, unfold, then flip the paper over.

step 2

fold the top left + top right edges in to meet the center line. then flip over.

step 3

fold those top edges to the center again, but only the top layer (don’t crease the flaps from the previous step).

step 4

fold the bottom edges up to the center line (you’ll get two small white triangles stacked).

step 5

fold the right flap down, lining up the reference point with the bottom edge.

step 6

repeat the same flap fold on the left side.

step 7

fold the top square section in half along the horizontal diagonal.

step 8

fold the top flap upward so the marked points line up.

step 9

fold the left and right edges of that top flap up to meet the top edge, then unfold (guide creases).

step 10

swivel-fold both sides inward toward the center, using the guide creases.

step 11

pleat the bottom corner to make the tail, then flip the model over.

step 12

pleat the tail again and tighten the body shape a bit.

step 13

shape the arms + legs by narrowing or pleating them for volume.
