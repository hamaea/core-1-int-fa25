bunnies are nice too

origami rabbit

soft ears, sharp creases. somehow very polite.

i've always thought it was so cute how they jump around in the grasses, but i always thought it was so sad how people hunted them so easily.

paper: 1 square sheet. thinner paper helps with the ears.

step 1

start with the white side up. fold the paper in half diagonally, then unfold.

step 2

fold the paper in half diagonally the other way, then unfold.

step 3

fold the paper in half horizontally, then unfold.

step 4

fold the left and right edges into the center crease.

step 5

collapse the model into a preliminary base (use the diagonal creases to squash it flat).

step 6

fold the top layer’s left and right edges into the center.

step 7

repeat step 6 on the back.

step 8

fold the top point downward slightly to form the rabbit’s head.

step 9

inside reverse fold the left and right points upward to create the ears.

step 10

fold the bottom point upward to shape the body.

step 11

turn the model over. slightly open and adjust the ears and head until it feels balanced.

step 12

slightly open and adjust the ears and head until it feels balanced.

fin.

free to hop around everywhere
