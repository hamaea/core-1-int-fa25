isn't it interesting that we love butterflies?

origami butterfly

it's interesting that we love butterflies, but hate moths. maybe because they eat holes in our clothes, but in my opinion i don't like either. i don't like bugs.

step 1

fold in half horizontally, crease well, then unfold.

step 2

fold in half vertically, crease well, then unfold.

step 3

fold the top quarter down to the center line along the dotted line. crease, then unfold.

step 4

fold the bottom quarter up to the center line along the dotted line. crease, then unfold.

step 5

fold the right quarter to the center line along the dotted line. crease, then unfold.

step 6

fold the left quarter to the center line along the dotted line. crease, then unfold.

step 7

fold the paper in half diagonally. crease well, then unfold.

step 8

fold diagonally the other way. crease well, then unfold.

step 9

turn the paper over so the colored side is facing up.

step 10

fold each corner to the center along the dotted line. crease well, then unfold (do all 4 corners).

step 11

turn the paper over so the white side is facing up again.

step 12

fold the two sides toward the center only along the middle half (don’t fold the top/bottom parts). then fold the top + bottom to the center along the dotted lines.

step 13

you should have a boat base now. fold the bottom two flaps down along the dotted lines.

step 14

flip the model over.

step 15

fold the top part down along the dotted line.

step 16

flip the model over again.

step 17

fold the top right flap toward the center along the dotted line (from top corner to halfway down the diagonal). repeat on the top left flap.

step 18

flip the model over.

step 19

valley fold along the dotted line in the arrow direction, crease, then unfold. repeat on the other side (symmetry matters).

step 20

pinch the middle along the last folds and make a mountain fold on the vertical center line to form the final butterfly shape.
