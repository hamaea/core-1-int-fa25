isn't it interesting that we love butterflies?

step 1

this is the front of our origami paper, the origami butterfly will end up being this color.

step 2

this is the back of the origami paper, start this side up.

step 3

fold the bottom right corner diagonally up to the top left corner.

step 4

unfold the previous step.

step 5

fold the bottom left corner diagonally up to the top right corner.

step 6

unfold the previous step.

step 7

fold the bottom edge up to the top edge.

step 8

unfold the previous step.

step 9

fold the right edge over to the left edge.

step 10

unfold the previous step.

step 11

fold the bottom right corner to the middle point.

step 12

now fold all of the other corners to the middle point.

step 13

flip the paper over to the other side.

step 14

fold the bottom corner to the middle point.

step 15

now fold all of the other corners to the middle point.

step 16

unfold the paper completely.

step 17

flip the paper over to the back, so the corner flaps are angled up from the table.

step 18

fold the left and right edges to the center.

step 19

bring both of the top flaps down and outwards.

step 20

flatten the top section so that it looks like the roof of a house.

step 21

rotate the paper and repeat on the other end.

step 22

flip the paper over to the other side.

step 23

fold the top section down to the bottom.

step 24

fold the front left and right flaps down to the bottom.

step 25

fold the outer diagonal edges of the lower flaps in a little bit as shown.

step 26

fold the left section over to the right.

step 27

to create the "body" of the butterfly, make a diagonal crease on the left edge.

step 28

unfold the previous two steps and flip the paper over to the front of the butterfly.

step 29

reverse the left crease you made in step 27 and shape the wings into the desired position.
