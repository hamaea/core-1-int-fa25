step 1

start with the white side up. fold in half, then unfold.

step 2

fold in half the other way, unfold again. then flip the paper over.

step 3

fold both diagonals, then unfold.

step 4

rotate the paper 45° counterclockwise, then flip it over.

step 5

collapse the creases into a square base (aka squash it into the neat little diamond).

step 6

lift the top-right flap and squash fold it flat.

step 7

flip the model over and repeat the squash fold on the other side.

step 8

repeat that squash fold on the two remaining flaps (so all four sides match).

step 9

fold the left + right edges into the centerline, then unfold (you’re just making guide creases).

step 10

use those creases to do a petal fold.

step 11

flip it over and do the same petal fold on the back.

step 12

repeat the petal fold on the remaining flaps until all sides are done.

step 13

rotate the whole model 180°.

step 14

fold the top triangle up, then gently pull the two top flaps apart to open that little triangle pocket.

step 15

push the center down and sink that triangle into the pocket (open sink).

step 16

flip over and repeat steps 14–15 on the other side.

step 17

repeat steps 14–15 on the remaining flaps too.

step 18

fold the top left + right edges into the centerline. flip and repeat on the back.

step 19

repeat that centerline edge fold on the remaining flaps.

step 20

inside reverse fold the top flaps along the dotted lines (flip and repeat on the back).

step 21

pull one pair of legs downward and flatten them.

step 22

mountain fold the legs downward along the dotted lines (shape them how you want).

step 23

blow gently into the hole to puff up the body.

now the head: grab your second sheet and make the same base again.

step 24

on a new sheet, repeat steps 1–12 to make the same frog-base setup.

step 25

fold the top triangular flap up. repeat on the other side.

step 26

flip the top-right flap to the left and repeat step 25. do this on the remaining flaps too.

step 27

pleat the left + right top flaps behind the front layers. flip and repeat (slightly higher angle).

step 28

fold the body + leg edges into the centerline, working top-down. make a swivel fold at the junction. repeat all around.

step 29

mountain fold the legs upward along the dotted lines.

step 30

insert the finished top half into the bottom half (fold the long flap in half first if it’s fighting you).
